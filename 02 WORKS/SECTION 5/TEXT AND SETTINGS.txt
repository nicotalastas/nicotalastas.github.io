[C7B299]So Nestly consolidates the whole process. Verified listings, direct landlord contact, and a roommate-matching system with ratings. So you're choosing a living situation with information instead of guesswork.

The roommate rating system is the part I care about most: it borrows the same accountability logic that makes Airbnb work, but points it sideways, between the people who'll actually be sharing a kitchen, not just at the property itself. I led ideation, branding, and the full wireframe, and brought in support to build it into a working app and handle the legal groundwork listings require. Nestly placed 2nd at CIIT's CesCon 2026, which was less a validation of the visuals than of the actual gap: enough people recognized the problem to reward a fix for it.[C7B299]
