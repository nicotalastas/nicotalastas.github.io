[232121]I kept noticing this pattern everywhere:
arguments, group chats, meetings.

So I took Shannon and Weaver's communication model and mapped it onto a real scenario )a couple texting through a trust conflict, tracing exactly where the signal left the sender, where interference got misread as intent, and where the receiver started decoding jealousy instead of the message that was actually sent. 

The scenario is romantic. The mechanism isn't. The same cycle which is to [232121][FF6943]respond before you understand, defend before you decode [FF6943][232121]plays out in boardrooms and group chats just as often as it does at 11:23 PM. [232121]
